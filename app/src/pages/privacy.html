Privacy Policy

Explaining data collection and protection

Last updated: December 15, 2024

Our Commitment: Pixels.Earth is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data and never sell your personal information to third parties.

Information We Collect

Data Type Purpose Storage
Wallet Address Authentication & transactions Blockchain (public)
Session Keys Temporary transaction signing Local storage only
Pixel Placements Core functionality Blockchain (public)
Usage Analytics Improve user experience PostHog (EU servers)

Blockchain Data

By using Pixels.Earth, you interact with the Solana blockchain. Please be aware:

  • All blockchain transactions are public and permanent
  • Your wallet address is visible on the blockchain
  • Pixel placements are associated with your wallet address
  • We cannot delete or modify blockchain data

Analytics

We use PostHog for privacy-focused analytics to understand how users interact with our platform. This helps us:

  • Improve the user interface and experience
  • Identify and fix bugs
  • Understand feature usage
  • Monitor platform performance

PostHog Privacy: Our analytics are hosted in the EU and comply with GDPR. We do not track personal identifying information beyond wallet addresses (which are public on the blockchain anyway).

Local Storage

We store the following data locally on your device:

  • Map Position: Your last viewed location for convenience
  • Session Keys: Temporary keys for faster transactions
  • Sound Preferences: Mute/unmute settings
  • RPC Settings: Custom RPC endpoints if configured
  • Location Cache: Cached reverse geocoding results

You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings.

Third-Party Services

Pixels.Earth integrates with:

  • Solana Blockchain: For pixel placement and shard ownership
  • MagicBlock Ephemeral Rollups: For high-speed transactions
  • OpenStreetMap: For map tile rendering
  • Nominatim: For location name lookup (rate-limited, no user data sent)
  • PostHog: For privacy-focused analytics

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • HTTPS encryption for all communications
  • No storage of private keys on our servers
  • Session keys are stored only in your browser
  • Regular security audits of our smart contracts

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View what data we have about you
  • Deletion: Clear local storage data (blockchain data cannot be deleted)
  • Opt-out: Disable analytics by using browser privacy features
  • Disconnect: Disconnect your wallet at any time

Children's Privacy

Pixels.Earth is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please reach out through our official channels.
